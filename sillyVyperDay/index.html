Welcome to Vyper Day Event, Turkey Fall 2023

Vyper Summit 2023

Istanbul Congress Center, Istanbul, Turkey

The Vyper Summit is a collaborative event focusing on the future of Vyper. We're excited to bring back Vyper Summit for another edition, again as part of the Devconnect week!

Speaker and attendee applications for 2023 are open!

Speak Attend

Registrations are open!

Tickets for the Vyper Day 2023 are free and given out on an application basis. Applications are open.

The Vyper Day is focused on an advanced audience and is not suitable for beginners who are just getting started with (Solidity) programming.

What will we talk about?

This year, presentations and discussions will revolve around the following topics:

  • Vyper and Yul
  • Language changes or extension proposals
  • Long-term future of Vyper (Verkle tree support, experimental Vyper, etc.)
  • Feedback on recently introduced features
  • Unification/extension of the ABI
  • Tooling, debuggers and debugging data output
  • Testing frameworks
  • Source verification
  • Layer 2 support, cross-chain support
  • Mechanisms/programming patterns (on the EVM) like mutexes, state machines, upgradeable contracts, libraries, create2, etc.
