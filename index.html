Smart contract development

Debugger

Inspect and fix smart contract bugs and production issues by using a fast and intuitive tool to analyze precise lines of code in a human-readable format.

Gas Profiler

Analyze gas usage per individual function calls, optimize smart contracts, and reduce costs with a built-in profiler that gives you in-depth information.

Simulator

Play out specific historical transactions and current transaction outcomes before sending them on-chain by changing relevant parameters and source code.

Forks

Test simple and complex smart contract scenarios on a best-in-class, real-time, zero-setup network that heavily relies on historical and up-to-date production data.

Alerting
