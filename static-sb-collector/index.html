About Silverback
Silverback lets you create and deploy your own Python bots that respond to on-chain events. The Silverback library leverages the Ape development framework as well as its ecosystem of plugins and packages to enable you to develop simple-yet-sophisticated automated applications that can listen and respond to live chain data. Silverback applications are excellent for use cases that involve continuously monitoring and responding to on-chain events, such as newly confirmed blocks or contract event logs.
